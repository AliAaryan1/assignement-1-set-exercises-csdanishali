Chocolate Chip Cookies

This is a simple and delicious recipe for classic chocolate chip cookies that are crispy on the edges and chewy in the middle.

Cupcake Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Check
Unsalted Butter, softened 1 cup
White Sugar 1 cup
Packed Brown Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 2 teaspoons
All-purpose Flour 3 cups
Baking Soda 1 teaspoon
Hot Water 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Semisweet Chocolate Chips 2 cups
Chopped Walnuts (optional) 1 cup

Note: You can adjust the quantity of chocolate chips and nuts according to your preference.

Cookies!

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.
  5. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

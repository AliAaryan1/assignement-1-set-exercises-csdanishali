The Big Bang Theory

Smart is the new sexy

Overview

The Big Bang Theory is an American television sitcom that premiered on CBS in 2007, running for 12 seasons until 2019.

The show follows a group of socially awkward but brilliant physicists and engineers - Leonard, Sheldon, Howard, and Raj - whose lives change when a free-spirited woman named Penny moves in across the hall.

Main Cast

  • Jim Parsons as Sheldon Cooper
  • Johnny Galecki as Leonard Hofstadter
  • Kaley Cuoco as Penny
  • Simon Helberg as Howard Wolowitz
  • Kunal Nayyar as Raj Koothrappali
  • Mayim Bialik as Amy Farrah Fowler
  • Melissa Rauch as Bernadette Rostenkowski-Wolowitz

Main Characters

Sheldon Cooper

A theoretical physicist with OCD tendencies, a genius-level IQ, and virtually no social skills.

Leonard Hofstadter

An experimental physicist who serves as the more "normal" member of the group and Sheldon's roommate.

Penny

The attractive, aspiring actress who lives across the hall and becomes the guys' link to the normal world.

Reviews & Awards

"A brilliant comedy that makes science cool and nerdiness endearing." - The Hollywood Reporter
"Jim Parsons' Sheldon Cooper is one of television's most original comic creations." - Variety

The show won 7 Emmy Awards, including 4 for Jim Parsons as Outstanding Lead Actor in a Comedy Series.
